Project Name - Catan

    This is a simplified version of catan. Its purpose is to trade with players 
and with the banker in order to collect things like development cards and basically 
try to reach 10 victory points or more points than any other player after 10 rounds.

Required Text Files

    leaderboard.Text

Compile and rounds

    g++ catanGameDriver.cpp Player.cpp Banker.cpp cardDeck.cpp Utils.cpp -g -std=c++11
